The origin of sneakers

Sneakers refer to a pair of sports shoes adapted for urban and everyday use. Derived from models designed for sport, they are appreciated for their comfort and style. In fact, sneakers are also fashion accessories that were used in the world of hip-hop in the 1970s and 1980s before becoming streetwear brands in the 2000s that spread into popular culture.
